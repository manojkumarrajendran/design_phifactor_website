Q1.How to download the iOS app?

Send a request email to support@phifactor.com with your mobile UDID details.
Note: PhiFactor app supports only on iPhone6s plus device.

Q2.How to get my device UDID?

Each iPhone has a Unique Device Identifier (UDID), which is a sequence of 40 letters and numbers that is specific to your device. Go to this link http://get.udid.io and follow the simple instruction to get your device UDID.

Q7.phiFactor portal application is compatible with all leading browsers?

Firefox is the recommended browser for phiFactor portal application, because it supports well to play the secured video content. Other browsers do not support the secured video content. Other than playing secured video, phiFactor portal application is compatible with all the leading browsers.

Q8.What are the iOS devices supported by phiFactor iOS application?

phifator iOS application supports only on iPhone6splus device, because it's designed to capture the 4kvideo for the data set analysis. So it won't support other iPhone devices.

Q9.Does the researcher needs HIPPA training to capture the video process and portal?

Yes. Please feel free to ask the hippa questions to support@phiFactor.com

Q10.Do we need to capture the video process in back or front camera?

It’s mandatory to capture the video process in back camera. Because 4k videos are important for analyze the facial features and process the algorithm components. Front camera is used only for the testing purpose for developers and researchers during the development phase.

Q11.How to disable the notifications?

While using the phiFactor app turns on “Don’t disturb” mode. Go to settings and turn on Do not Disturb mode. It helps to continuous the video recording without any interruption and to avoid the unwanted noise during recording.

Q12.Do I need high speed network connection to work with phiFactor iOS app ?

Yes, It’s mandatory to have good wifi network or 4g to upload the recorded 4K video files to cloud storage.

Q13.Shall I allow the party to wear spectacle while recording the video?

No. Algorithm doesn’t work well if the party wears spectacle. Its mandatory to avoid the spectacle while collecting the dataset videos.

Q14. Shall I allow the party to cover his face mask,hair or any accessories?

Q15. Did I allowed to share my credentials to friends/colleague?

No. It’s against HIPPA standards. So please don’t share the credentials to anyone.

Q16.What should I do if my password expires after some time?

Password is valid for only 90 days, it’s mandatory to reset the your password for every 90 days once. The system will prompt you to change your passwords at the expiry of 90th day. In this case you will be permitted access to your account only after changing the password.

Q17.What should i do if my account is blocked ?

Account will be blocked after 5 invalid attempts of login. In case your account blocked then contact support@phifactor.com to enable to account.

Q18.What will happen if the account hasn’t been used for longer times?

Unused accounts will be blocked at the expiry of 90th day. Email notification will be sent to the user at the time of 80th day,subsequent email followed 87th day and then the account will be blocked at the expiry of 90th day. In this case user should contact support@phifactor.com to enable his the account

Q19.What are the quality checks to be followed for video capturing?

Please refer the instruction video given in the app for the quality checks needs to be followed while recording the video also follow the on time instruction messages shown on screen while record the each video. Once the quality metric meets the video recording process will start automatically and ends on the predefined duration automatically.

Q20.How to validate the captured videos?

All the captured video will be stored in the secured environment,the videos/patient details can be accessed through https://portal.phifactor.com.
